Book Appointment

Choose from our convenient appointment options:

Online Appointment

Book your appointment online through our website. Use our online platform to schedule appointments at your convenience.

Offline Appointment

To schedule an appointment, simply visit our hospital in person. Our welcoming staff will assist you in booking an appointment with your preferred doctor. We look forward to providing you with the care you need.

Telecommunication Appointment

Consult with our doctors remotely via video call. For remote consultations, we provide telecommunication appointments.

Our Services

Explore the range of services we offer:

Lab Tests

Get convenient lab testing with our home service option, where trained professionals collect samples at your doorstep. For comprehensive testing, visit our well-equipped lab, staffed by experienced professionals ensuring accurate and timely results. Choose the comfort of your home or our advanced facility for all your diagnostic needs.

Telemedicine Services

We offer telemedicine services, enabling patients to consult healthcare providers remotely. Through virtual appointments, patients can receive medical advice, prescriptions, and follow-up care without visiting the hospital in person. Telemedicine enhances accessibility to healthcare, particularly for those with mobility constraints or living in remote areas, ensuring timely and convenient medical support.
